DINNER TONIGHT PRIVACY POLICY

Dinner Tonight AU does not collect, store or share any personal information about you. Recipes, shopping lists and meal planners are stored in your iCloud with a backup stored locally on your device for offline mode to work for shopping lists and meal planners. Sharing recipes, lists and planners is managed through iCloud.

Dinner Tonight AU notes that there is the ability to add an external link to another website / video for each recipe. These sites are not owned by Dinner Tonight AU and, as such, have no control over and assume no responsibility for their content and privacy policies whatsoever.

When importing ingredients from photos, the information is processed on the device only and immediately deleted.

Our Privacy policy may be updated occasionally as required and would recommend periodically reviewing any changes. We will notify users in App for any major changes

You can remove all of your recipes, lists and planners at any time by deleting the data for Dinner Tonight AU on your iCloud account.

If you have any queries about the Privacy Policy or how your data is managed, please contact us at dinnertonightau@gmail.com.
